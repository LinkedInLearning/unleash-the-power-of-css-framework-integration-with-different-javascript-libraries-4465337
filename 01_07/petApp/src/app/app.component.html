What the BMI result means

A BMI range of 18.5 to 24.9 is considered a 'healthy weight.' Maintaining a healthy weight may lower your pet's chances of experiencing health issues later on, such as random illness and other viral disease. Aim for a nutritious diet for your pets with reduced fat and well cooked foods, adding bones, milk and eggs. Additionally. Strive for regular physical walk with your pet, ideally about 30 minutes daily for five days a week.

Healthy eating

Healthy eating promotes weight control, disease prevention, better digestion, immunity, and mood.

Regular exercise

Exercise improves fitness, aids weight control, elevates mood, and reduces disease risk, fostering wellness and activeness.

Adequate sleep

Sleep enhances mental clarity, emotional stability, and physical wellness, promoting overall restoration and rejuvenation.
